Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 small onion
  • Soy sauce
  • 100ml de whipping cream
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken breasts into cubes and 1 small onion into strips
  2. Season with:
    • Garlic powder
    • Sweet chili
    • Basil
    • Coriander
    • Oregon
    • Salt
    • Ground black pepper
  3. Coat the chicken well in seasoning
  4. Put olive oil in the frying pan and preheat
  5. Put the chicken in the frying pan
  6. After the chicken is cooked, add soy sauce to taste. and the 100ml of whipping cream
  7. Colocar a cebola previamente cortada e mexer bem
  8. Taste and adjust the flavor with the necessary seasonings
