Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Indulge in the creamy, savory goodness of Classic Spaghetti Carbonara.

This Italian pasta dish is a timeless favorite that combines al dente spaghetti, crispy pancetta, creamy eggs, and grated Pecorino Romano cheese for a satisfying and comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • 340g spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 115g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Pasta
  2. Crisp the Pancetta
  3. Prepare the Creamy Sauce
  4. Toss and Combine
  5. Garnish and Serve
